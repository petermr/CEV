0: Extraction of the essential oil from the aerial part of the tree was carried out by steaming with a Clevenger-type apparatus [ 20], in a 5-liter balloon containing 2 liters of distilled water.
1: The extractions were repeated three times in order to recover considerable volumes.
2: The essential oil obtained has been stored in small, opaque bottles and placed in a refrigerator at 4AC until used for testing antioxidant and antibacterial activities.
