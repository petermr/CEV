0: The GC-FID-MS analyses were performed using the Trace GC Ultra apparatus (Thermo Electron Corporation, Waltham, MA, USA) with FID and MS DSQ II detectors and the FID-MS splitter (SGE).
1: Operating conditions: apolar capillary column Rtx-1ms (Restek), 60 m A 0.25 mm i.d., film thickness 0.25 Am; temperature program, 50a300 AC at 4 AC/min; SSL injector temperature 280 AC; FID temperature 300 AC; split ratio 1:20; carrier gas helium at a regular pressure 200 kPa.
2: Mass spectra were acquired over the mass range 30a400 Da, ionization voltage 70 eV; ion source temperature 200 AC.
